About Vantage

We Are a Team of
Builders and Thinkers

Founded in 2019, Vantage started with a single conviction: small businesses deserve the same caliber of digital strategy as the biggest brands in the world.

Our Story

What began as a two-person freelance operation out of a spare bedroom has grown into a 12-person studio serving clients across three continents. We have worked with bootstrapped startups, family businesses, and funded scale-ups — and every engagement has taught us something new.

Our philosophy has never changed: strategy first, aesthetics second, results always. We measure our success entirely by the growth of the clients we serve.
